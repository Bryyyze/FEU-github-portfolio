Other Projects

Tusok-Tusok

Tusok-Tusok is a simple Unity game based on Filipino street food culture. It’s a light and casual game where players try to “tusok” fishballs at the right time. This project helped me practice basic Unity gameplay mechanics and design.

3D Modeling: Sci-fi Kunai

A beginner 3D modeling project made in Autodesk Maya. I created a simple sci-fi inspired kunai to explore basic shapes, textures, and materials. This helped me learn how to model and render small objects in Maya.

3D Modeling: Sci-fi Environment

A collaborative 3D Sci-Fi Environment created in Autodesk Maya, where I served as the team leader. I directed the overall layout, lighting setup, and scene composition to ensure a cohesive visual style and technical quality across all assets. This project highlights teamwork, leadership, and environmental design skills in a futuristic setting.

3D Modeling: Sci-fi Wall

A small 3D wall model made in Autodesk Maya. I focused on learning how to shape objects, add simple details, and make them look like part of a sci-fi scene. This project helped me understand the basics of structure and design for environment pieces.
