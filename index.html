About Me

Hi! I’m Bryan Robin M. Santiago, a passionate Information Technology student at FEU Alabang specializing in Animation and Game Development. I have hands-on experience in Unity, where I’ve worked on both 2D and 3D games, focusing on designing and implementing user interfaces, gameplay systems, and interactive mechanics.

My passion for games goes beyond playing — I love discussing, developing, and experimenting with creative ideas that bring stories and cultures to life. I also have basic knowledge in 3D modeling, allowing me to contribute to both the artistic and technical aspects of game development.

I aim to create meaningful and culturally inspired games that engage players through unique visuals, storytelling, and gameplay experiences.
