Solano: The Lightbearer

Solano is a collaborative game developed by a four-member team over the course of one and a half months. I contributed as the UI/UX programmer, supporting gameplay programmer, and sound/music designer. My responsibilities included implementing` intuitive user interfaces, improving overall player experience, assisting with key gameplay systems, and creating the game’s sound effects and music. Although developed under a tight timeline, Solano represents our team’s dedication and creativity in bringing a unique concept to life.
